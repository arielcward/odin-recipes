Sautéed Green Beans

Description

I created this cilantro-lime dressing after having a delicious grilled chicken salad at TGI Friday's. I was determined to figure out how to make their cilantro-lime dressing, and this is what I came up with. Serve it over a hearty green salad topped with grilled chicken.

  • 1 medium jalapeno pepper, seeded and coarsely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • ⅓ cup honey
  • ¼ cup lime juice
  • ¼ cup packed cilantro leaves
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon salt, or to taste
  • ½ cup extra-virgin olive oil

Steps

  1. Pulse jalapeño, garlic, and ginger in a food processor until finely chopped.Taste and season with salt if needed before serving.
  2. Add honey, lime juice, cilantro, vinegar, and salt; pulse 3 to 4 times to blend.
  3. Pour oil in slowly, with the processor running, until dressing is smooth.
  4. Taste and season with salt if needed before serving.
